Our Story

I’m a homemaker from Pandaravadai, Thanjavur, dedicated to preserving the art of Aari embroidery. Every blouse, saree, and lehenga is handcrafted with patience and love — taking 30–50 hours per piece. I take only a handful of orders each month to ensure unmatched quality. Alongside, I offer natural handmade henna cones as a small extension of my craft, crafted with care and love. At BISMI, tradition isn’t just preserved—it’s celebrated 💫

Why Henna Cones?

100% natural, handmade henna cones from Thanjavur, crafted with care for lasting, safe, and beautiful designs. Perfect for personal use, festivals, or professional artists.

Why Aari Embroidery?

Aari embroidery transforms any blouse or saree into a one-of-a-kind statement piece. Each stitch is crafted by hand over many hours, ensuring elegance that lasts and stands out. I accept only a limited number of orders each month to maintain the highest standard of craftsmanship.
