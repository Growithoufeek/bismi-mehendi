Our Story

At BISMI Aari & Mehendi Center, we celebrate the elegance of Pure Hand embroidery and Artistry Every blouse, saree, and lehenga is adorned with exquisite Aari work, stitched patiently by hand to bring unmatched detail and grace—perfect for brides and festive wear. Alongside our embroidery, we also offer fresh, handmade henna cones , crafted with the same love and care. At BISMI, tradition isn’t just preserved—it’s celebrated 💫
